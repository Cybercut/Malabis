ABOUT US

Malabis was founded with the goal of streamlining the laundry cleaning process. Our goal was to create a more convenient, transparent, and simple solution to our customers who just want a simple and efficient laundry service in Bengaluru. We realized that our friends and family had the same frustrations with their dirty laundry all around Bengaluru. They didn’t have time, the laundry process was a hassle, and the market was quite niche. Malabis was created with these problems in mind, and offers excellent communication, a transparent pricing model, and thoroughly vetting all of our laundry delivery teams.

The mission of Malabis was to change the way laundry cleaning service was done in Bengaluru. You used to have to visit laundry service shops that are far away and have uneven pricing rates. We have transformed that model by offering flat rate pricing that has no surprises and lets you book online in 60 seconds.

Lastly, we hire the best cleaners that Bengaluru has to offer. We know that inviting somebody to receive your laundry is a big deal, and we have all of our cleaners go through a detailed vetting process before they are sent into your house. Each agent is experienced, background checked, and insured. If anything were to happen to your clothing, you will be refunded and refinanced. We are so confident in our cleaners, that we have a 100% satisfaction guarantee – if you find something that they missed, we will reclean for free. We strive to make this process as simple as possible for our customers, and would love the opportunity to simplify your life! If you have any questions, please see our contact page for an email address and phone number. We look forward to being your new cleaning service in Columbus!
